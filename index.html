// The Challenge
$0B

The MSME credit gap in India. 63 million small businesses. Most can't access formal credit. The financial system wasn't built for them.

Traditional lending relies on collateral and credit scores that small businesses don't have. The result — businesses with real revenue and genuine need are shut out of the formal financial system.

We're building the bridge →
// How It Works

The Kubar Protocol

Connecting MSMEs to credit, embedded where they work.

Step 01

Connect

Plug into existing MSME platforms and business systems via our API. Seamless integration with zero disruption.

Step 02

Match

AI-powered lender matching based on business profile, sector, and risk assessment across 50+ lending partners.

Step 03

Disburse

Fast approval and disbursement through our network of banks and NBFCs. Credit delivered in under 48 hours.

integration.js 
const kubar = new KubarSDK({ apiKey: 'kb_live_...' });

const match = await kubar.credit.findMatch({
  gstin:  'XXXXXXXXXXXX',
  amount: 500000,
  sector: 'manufacturing'
});

// → { lender: 'HDFC', rate: '10.5%', approved: true }
