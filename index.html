Credit Intelligence

Making the Data Speak Clearly

We build systems that help lenders understand what businesses truly are — not just what their balance sheets say.

Turning Information into Understanding

  • Applies NLP contextualisation layer to quantitative data to help lenders understand what your business earns and how it operates.
  • Combines data analytics with human understanding for fair, quick, and accurate loan decisions.
  • Blends quantitative rigour with qualitative depth for insights that actually make sense.

Lending That Fits the Industry

  • Replaces one-size-fits-all lending with industry-tailored assessment models.
  • Incorporates specialized parameters for diverse sectors — textile vs. ball bearing manufacturing.
  • Analyses sector-specific cash flow patterns, supply chains, and growth metrics.

Manage the Entire Loan Journey

  • Digitises and executes agreements via DLT-powered smart contracts.
  • Streamlines fund movements through eNACH mandates and UPI auto-debit.
  • Monitors asset performance with Account Aggregator integrations.

Understanding Business Beyond Numbers

  • Ingests structured data from invoices, GST filings, transactions, and supplier payments.
  • Captures unstructured inputs including handwritten notes and informal business documents.
  • Transforms fragmented raw inputs into standardised quantitative insights.

Why It Works

Traditional credit models rely on paperwork and assumptions. We rely on proof.

NavDhan uses real-time signals from business operations to assess creditworthiness more accurately than traditional bureau-only scores.

  • Unified validation from GST, Account Aggregator, and marketplace sources.
  • Sector-specific scoring models built for manufacturing, trading, and services.
  • Embedded disbursal flows through eNACH, UPI, and DLT-powered smart contracts.
  • Continuous monitoring for better risk visibility and healthier portfolios.
